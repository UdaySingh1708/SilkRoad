Community Guidelines

Silk Road Chat is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and enjoyable anonymous chat experience for everyone.

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, discrimination, and threats are not allowed.

No Illegal Activity

Do not use Silk Road Chat for illegal activities, fraud, scams, or any activity that violates applicable laws.

No Adult or Explicit Content

Do not share sexually explicit content, nudity, or exploitative material.

Protect Your Privacy

Never share passwords, banking information, government IDs, home addresses, or other sensitive personal information.

Report Abuse

If another user violates these guidelines, use the Report button during chat so we can investigate.

Enforcement

Users who violate these guidelines may be blocked or permanently restricted from using Silk Road Chat.
