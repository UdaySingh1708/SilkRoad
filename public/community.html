Community Guidelines

Silk Road Chat is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and enjoyable anonymous chat experience for everyone.

Age Requirement

Silk Road Chat is available only for users who are 14 years or older. By using the platform, you confirm that you meet the minimum age requirement and agree to follow these Community Guidelines.

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, discrimination, threats, or abusive behavior are not allowed.

No Illegal Activity

Do not use Silk Road Chat for illegal activities, fraud, scams, or any activity that violates applicable laws.

No Adult or Explicit Content

Do not share sexually explicit content, nudity, or exploitative material. Any inappropriate content may result in immediate restrictions.

Protect Your Privacy

Never share passwords, banking information, government IDs, home addresses, or other sensitive personal information with strangers.

Report Abuse

If another user violates these guidelines, use the Report button during chat so our moderation system can review the issue.

Blocking Users

You can block users who make you uncomfortable. Blocked users will no longer be matched with you.

Enforcement

Users who violate these guidelines may be blocked, restricted, or permanently removed from Silk Road Chat.
