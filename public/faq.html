Frequently Asked Questions

What is Silk Road Chat?

Silk Road Chat is a free anonymous platform that lets you instantly connect with random people through video, voice, and text chat.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can start chatting without creating an account.

Is Silk Road Chat free?

Yes. Basic anonymous chatting is completely free.

Is my identity shared?

No. Silk Road Chat is designed to keep users anonymous. However, never share personal information with strangers.

How do I report someone?

Use the Report button during a chat session if another user violates our Community Guidelines.

Can I use Silk Road Chat on my phone?

Yes. Silk Road Chat works on modern desktop and mobile browsers.

Why can't my camera or microphone be detected?

Make sure you've granted your browser permission to access your camera and microphone and that you're using a supported browser.
