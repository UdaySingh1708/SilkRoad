Safety Guidelines

Silk Road is designed to help people connect anonymously. Please follow these safety rules while using the platform.

Protect Your Privacy

Never share personal information such as your full name, address, phone number, passwords, banking details, or private documents with strangers.

Respect Other Users

Do not harass, threaten, bully, or abuse other people. Treat everyone with respect.

Report Unsafe Behavior

If someone violates the rules or makes you uncomfortable, use the Report button to notify us.

Be Careful With Links

Do not open suspicious links or download files sent by strangers.

Age Requirement

Users should only use Silk Road if they meet the legal age requirements applicable in their location.

Stay Safe

Anonymous chatting can be fun, but always use good judgement when interacting online.
