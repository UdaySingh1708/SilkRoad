Safety Guidelines

Silk Road Chat is designed to help people connect anonymously. Please follow these safety rules while using the platform.

Age Requirement

Silk Road Chat is available only for users who are 14 years or older. By using Silk Road Chat, you confirm that you meet this minimum age requirement.

Protect Your Privacy

Never share personal information such as your full name, address, phone number, passwords, banking details, government IDs, or private documents with strangers.

Respect Other Users

Do not harass, threaten, bully, discriminate against, or abuse other people. Treat every user with respect.

No Inappropriate Content

Do not share nudity, sexual content, illegal material, or any content that may harm other users. Violations may result in restrictions or removal.

Report Unsafe Behavior

If someone violates the rules or makes you uncomfortable, use the Report button during chat so the issue can be reviewed.

Block Users When Needed

Use the Block button if you do not want to communicate with a specific user again.

Be Careful With Links

Do not open suspicious links or download files sent by strangers. They may contain harmful content.

Stay Safe Online

Anonymous chatting can be enjoyable, but always use good judgment when interacting with people online. Never arrange meetings or share private information without proper safety precautions.
